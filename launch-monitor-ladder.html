Key notes for Canadians:
• Bushnell LPi & Launch Pro — US-only devices. They require a US internet connection every 45 days or they stop working entirely. Not recommended for Canadian buyers.
• Foresight GC3S — globally available (unlike Bushnell variants); sold at foresightsports.ca and par2pro.com. Same hardware as GC3 but subscription-locked.
• Foresight GC3 — the "buy once" option; fully unlocked, no region lock, no sub. Sold via foresightsports.ca. Pricey but the gold standard under $10K for accuracy.
• Eye Mini Core* — Amazon.com exclusive; must import to Canada (~$1,400 CAD at $999 USD holiday price; ~$2,100 CAD regular). Holiday deal ran Nov–Dec 2025.
• Putting: Square, SkyTrak+, Eye Mini Lite/Core/Mini, GC3/GC3S, R50, ProTee VX all support sim putting. Rapsodo MLM2 Pro and Mevo Gen2 do not.
• Trackman 4 sits behind the golfer on the floor — not overhead. It tracks actual full ball flight rather than estimating it, which is why it's the Tour standard. But indoors, camera-based units like the GC3 and EYE XO2 get very close in accuracy at a fraction of the price. The Trackman iO is their ceiling-mount indoor version — but club data is a separate $9,500 USD unlock.
• CAD prices are estimates — confirm with Canadian dealers: Leo's Golf, Par2Pro, Great North Golf, SimCaddy, foresightsports.ca.
